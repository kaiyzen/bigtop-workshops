What is a Bigtop Workshop?

The Apache Bigtop project is setting the standard for testing, packaging and integration of leading Big Data components. Bigtop Workshops are typically single day events where members of the Apache Bigtop community get toghether to share work and ideas in the areas data science and big data engeering.
